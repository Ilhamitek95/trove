How it works

From kitchen table to shopfront, in four steps.

i

Tell us about your craft

A short form about you and what you make — written like a chat, not paperwork. A real person reads every application, usually within a day or two.

ii

Add your pieces

Give each piece a name, a price and a few honest words. The form guides you step by step — three or four pieces is a lovely start.

iii

iv

Good to know

Your questions, answered honestly.

I've never sold online before — is that okay?+
That's exactly who trove is built for. Your shop dashboard is a simple checklist — add a piece, see your orders, mark them ready — with no jargon anywhere. If you can post a photo to Instagram, you can run a trove shop.
Do I need a trade licence?+
No. Trove buys your pieces from you and resells them to shoppers, so you can sell here without any licence. If you do have one, mention it when you apply — it unlocks extra payout options as you grow.
What does it cost?+
Nothing to join — no monthly fee, no listing fee. You decide each piece's price. When one sells, Trove buys it from you at 80% of that price, and that's the whole arrangement. If nothing sells, you owe nothing.
How does delivery work?+
You don't deliver anything. When a piece sells, our courier collects it from your door and takes it to the buyer — you just have it wrapped and ready. You can follow each order's journey in your dashboard.
How and when do I get paid?+
Weekly, to the bank account you add in your dashboard. A sale becomes payable once the piece is delivered and the buyer's return window closes — your Payments page shows exactly what's coming and when.
What if I only make a few pieces a month?+
Small-batch is the point of trove. A shop with four lovely pieces is very welcome — and the application asks how many orders a month you're comfortable with, so you're never overwhelmed.
Can I keep selling on Instagram or at markets?+
Of course. Your trove shop is another shelf for your work, not an exclusive deal — keep selling wherever your customers already find you.
